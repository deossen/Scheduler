Welcome to the La trobe Staff Scheduling System

Our system is designed to help manage the scheduling of staff at the university. With our easy-to-use interface, you can quickly view the current schedule, add new staff members, remove staff members, and update the schedule as needed.

Our system is ideal for administrators who are responsible for managing the schedules of university staff, as well as for individual staff members who need to view their schedules or make changes.
